Description

Spin Counter is an app where users compete to see who's the best at spinning their phone. It's inspired by games such as Flappy Bird that are addictive because of their low learning curves, short turn lengths, and frustration.

Features

  • Single Player Mode
  • 2 Player Bluetooth Brawl
  • Global Rankings
  • Friends List
